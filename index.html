KISHI MUNKISHIRA

A cultural registry connecting a private physical mythic card collection to a public digital scarcity layer on the Polygon network.

What This Is

Munkidori is a private physical collection of mythic cards curated by its creator. Each artifact represents a cultural entry within a growing archive.

KISHI Munkishira functions as the economic and digital layer of this archive. Its supply reflects the expansion of the physical collection through a permanent on-chain reduction mechanism.

How Scarcity Is Created

1. Physical Acquisition

A new Munkidori card is added to the creator’s private collection.

2. Cultural Registration

The acquisition is documented within the public cultural registry.

3. On-Chain Reduction

A corresponding quantity of KISHI is permanently removed from circulation through an irreversible blockchain transaction.
